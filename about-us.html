About Us

Anaya Honey is 100% pure, sustainably and ethically harvested from our own apiary and from Zimbabwean women living in rural areas. We take pride in delivering wholesome honey and honey related products efficiently to our customers- whom we value. We take pride in being one of the Honey cultivators.

The Founders

Hello. We’re Kuda and Fungi, proud founders of Anaya Honey – the House of Wellness. We’ve been keeping Bees since we were toddlers, so you can say, Beekeeping is second nature to us. From an early age, Honey production allowed us to get that extra dollar in our pocket. In 2015, like a Eureka moment, the idea of getting rural women into Beekeeping came to us. Rural women are key for achieving real economic, social and environmental transformation. Beekeeping provided an avenue for these women to increase their income, improve the wellbeing of their families and communities at large.

Women In Beekeeping

Facilitating and empowering women through beekeeping initiatives to allow women gain a source of income and sense of fulfilment.
